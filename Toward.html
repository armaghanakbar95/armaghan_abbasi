Towards Net Zero Internet of Things (IoT) System

üìò Project Overview

Internet of Things come up as game-changing topic following which the real-time monitoring, automation, and data-based decisions in even various industries like agriculture,manufacturing, environmental management, and smart cities can be achieved. IoT systems enable a smooth connection between the physical and the digital world by interconnecting sensors, wireless transmission, and cloud- based analytics. But as development of these systems is spreading at a very high pace, there is a growing demand to assure that the solutions of IoT are not only intelligent and scalable but also energy-efficient and environment friendly. Due to the international initiatives towards the minimization of carbon emissions and shifting towards the net-zero power consumption, developing low-power, the long range IoT systems has become a priority. The environmental or industrial monitoring apps used remotely, including air quality monitoring, water resource management, or machinery diagnostics often have to deploy sensors to the places where the reliable power infrastructure is unavailable. Therefore, it has to be a solution that is going to be low-powered, wide-area coverage communication, and also needs to be cloud integrated to get the data and also the decision process as well.
